Why These Changes Work
Consistency: Standardizing spacing (e.g., 12px between sidebar sections, 8px between labels and fields) eliminates jarring transitions and creates a cohesive design.
Readability: Increasing the spacing between labels and fields (from 5px to 8px) and between rows (from 15px to 20px) makes the content easier to scan.
Balance: Reducing oversized gaps (e.g., title to section from 30px to 20px) and adding padding ensures the layout feels proportionate without wasting space.
